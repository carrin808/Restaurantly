CHOOSE YOUR NSK EXPERIENCE WHAT’S YOURS GOING TO BE?

Our eatery offers guests healthy and delicious food choices with a huge focus on fresh, organic, and quality ingredients. From classic brunch favourites to bold and unique signature dishes, Spilt Milk offers simply inspired, handcrafted fare in a relaxed and welcoming setting.

Tucked away in a cozy corner of our expansive ground floor, join us for your freshly baked morning pastry, lunchtime bubbles, or a casual evening meal. Whatever you’re searching for, our skillfully prepared, diverse menu, locally roasted coffee, refined wines, and beautifully served cocktails, promise a wholly memorable experience any time of day.

Delhi

From the cobblestone sidewalks of Lisbon to the winemaking regions of Douro and Alentejo, Library 68 offers guests a slice of heavenly Portugal in Nairobi. Day or night, indulge in our intimate setting, delectable petiscos, charcuterie boards, and inventive bar snacks. We are passionate about bringing a Portuguese respect for ingredients to you - not to forget our unrivaled wine list. Because everything is better with wine.

Unwind under the glow of vintage lighting or share a chilled bottle (or two!) with friends in our outdoor courtyard, perfect for warm summer days. Whatever your mood, Library 68’s comfortable, yet trendy environment makes it an ideal spot for after-work drinks, a special celebration, or boozy catch-ups alike.

Sociali

Welcome to Social, where guests come to unwind with a handcrafted signature cocktail, karaoke it out with local live bands, or catch up on all the big games with their friends and fellow fans. As evening progresses, the lights are dimmed, the music pulses through the air, and you can dine and drink to your heart’s content on our iconic rooftop patio until all your worries breeze away.

The indoor adaptable venue can also be hired exclusively for various functions - be it an intimate wedding, a product launch for your newest innovation, or a next-level corporate event. Whatever you’re looking for, Social offers elevated views and vibes, so come here to let your hair down, make new friends, and escape ordinary life below.

Italian

Fire & Dough offers hand-tossed, artisan pizzas cooked right in front of you in our open kitchen, which also happens to be a container! Our homemade dough and fresh, natural ingredients come together masterfully over hand-stoked fires, and our creativity and respect for authenticity result in true pizza perfection: aromatic, piping hot, and perfectly crusted.

Spanning part of our large outdoor terrace, Fire & Dough evokes the nostalgic ambiance of sunny Naples, perfect for long, extended lunches with loved ones or a romantic dinner date. The friendly atmosphere, delightful showmanship, and comforting aromas set the mood for high energy fun and entertainment.

B Bunns

At Butter’d Buns, we believe in simplicity and wholesome, comforting food. Our entire burger menu is made in-house daily using our custom blend of high-quality beef, innovative flat-press cooking techniques, freshly baked buns, gourmet fries, and homemade sauces, bursting with flavour!

It’s not just our food that is genuine: it’s the people - humble, welcoming, honest, and direct. Like proud mums, we make everything from scratch and with love. If you’re looking for an affordable and enjoyable dining experience, we promise you hand-crafted, delicious burgers, fresh toppings, and a good dose of guilty pleasure!
